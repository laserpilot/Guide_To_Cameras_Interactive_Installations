Another important thing to keep in mind is that while many artistically geared software environments do work off-the-shelf with various cameras and input types (typically with UVC, more exotic cameras aren't always plug and play. Make sure you do your research before making a big purchase. If the tool doesn't exist to pull in a video feed, there are a few technologies that can route video between applications or even over the network. The most common tools are Syphon for macOS and Spout for Windows, but there are other options out there as well.
